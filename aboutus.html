About us


Established in 2021, Sur National Foodstuff Company is a specialized distributor of Thai & Fillipino products in Kuwait. Specialized in finding the perfect food ingredients for your high-quality meals.

We are authorized distributor for various brands like Monde M.Y. San, LIGO, Silver Swan, Phil Supreme, Dagupan, Aroy-D , Cock, Mother Choice, Pigeon, Thai Food king, Mama Sitas,in Kuwait.

We also deal in Consuladated products, Retail Co Ops, Key account, Wholesale, Groceries, Restaurant Supply ( Horeca ) & bakeries.

We aspire to be amongst the top leaders in the segment of Thai & Fillipino products and introduce new products for the total satisfaction of our customers.

Our team dedicate themselves to meet all challenges, present new growth and to continue to represent our products with best quality and thereby putting us in the best possible position within the FMCG market.

Sur National Foodstuff Co. facilitates warehouse and fleet of delivery vehicles.



Sur National Foodstuff Co.was established in 2021 as a privately owned company, with an intent to supply quality foodstuff to our consumers particularly the expatriates in Kuwait, making available at hand the food from their homeland.

We are one of the leading distributors of Filipino and Thai products in Kuwait ,specialized in finding the perfect food ingredients for your high quality meals. We have a dedicated & experienced sales team in oriental foods for each channel.

We are on way to pinnacle of success after the company expanded its operation by importing food product from Philipine and Thailand as an exclusive disributor of internatinaly famous brand in Kuwait.

Our mission is provide quality services to operate our food division of high ethical standards and quality service as we build long term relationship with our customers,employees,and vendors.

Within a short span of our operations, we are proud to taste that we are one of the leading foodstuff company in the state of Kuwait.

We are regular importers of various foodstuff from Philippine, Thailand and India.

We are nominated distributors for several food manufacturing companies from Philippine, Thailand and India , Apart from the above ,we also import regularly assorted food stuff from our consolidators from worldwide.

We stock hundreds of imported Filipino & Thai products ranging from the traditional fish product based , biscuits ,snack ,fruit preserve , sardines, canned foods, sauces , noodles and much more.

We offer a personalized customer experience to maximize satisfaction and to efficiently anticipate and react to our customers needs.
